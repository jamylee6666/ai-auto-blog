## Step-by-Step: Create Your Full Presentation in 15 Minutes

### Step 1: Define Your Presentation Topic & Outline (2 minutes)

Before you touch any tool, decide:
- **Topic:** What's your presentation about? (e.g., "Q3 Sales Report," "Product Launch Strategy," "Client Onboarding Overview")
- **Audience:** Who are you presenting to? (e.g., executive team, clients, internal team)
- **Key message:** What's the main takeaway? (e.g., "We exceeded targets by 15%")
- **Approximate slides needed:** How many? (e.g., 8–12 slides)

Write this down in a simple note. Example:
```
Topic: Q3 Sales Performance Review
Audience: Sales team + regional managers
Key message: Strong growth in APAC, challenges in EMEA
Slides needed: ~10
```

### Step 2: Generate Your Outline with ChatGPT or Claude (3 minutes)

Open **ChatGPT** or **Claude** and paste this prompt (customize as needed):

```
Create a professional presentation outline for: [YOUR TOPIC]

Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
Key message: [YOUR KEY MESSAGE]
Number of slides: [NUMBER]

For each slide, provide:
1. Slide title
2. 3–4 key talking points (bullet format)
3. Visual suggestion (e.g., chart, image type, icon)

Format it as a numbered list so I can copy it easily.
```

**Example output you'll get:**
```
1. Title Slide
   - Talking points: Title, Date, Your Name
   - Visual: Company logo + professional background

2. Agenda
   - Talking points: Overview of 3 main topics, timeline
   - Visual: Simple numbered list with icons

3. Q3 Revenue Highlights
   - Talking points: Total revenue, YoY growth %, key regions
   - Visual: Bar chart or trend line
...
```

**Copy this output.** You'll use it in the next step.

### Step 3: Upload Your Outline to Gamma AI (4 minutes)

Go to **gamma.app** and sign up (free or paid).

1. Click **"New Presentation"**
2. Select **"Create from text"** (or "AI Mode")
3. **Paste your entire outline** from Step 2 into the text box
4. Add a brief description in the **"Describe your presentation"** field:
   ```
   Q3 Sales Performance Review for sales team. 
   Include revenue metrics, regional breakdown, 
   and action items. Professional, data-driven style.
   ```
5. Click **"Generate"** or **"Create Presentation"**

Gamma will auto-generate:
- Slide layouts
- Basic design formatting
- Visual placeholders for charts and images

**This takes 2–3 minutes while the AI processes.**

### Step 4: Review and Edit Generated Slides (4 minutes)

Once Gamma finishes, you'll see a complete deck. Now:

1. **Review each slide** for accuracy
   - Check that titles match your outline
   - Verify talking points are clear and concise
   - Confirm the flow makes sense

2. **Quick edits:**
   - Click any slide to edit text
   - Fix data, numbers, or company names
   - Delete slides you don't need (e.g., if Gamma created 15 but you wanted 10)

3. **Add your data:**
   - Replace placeholder percentages with real metrics
   - Insert your company name where needed
   - Update date fields

**Pro tip:** Leave images as placeholders for now—you'll add them in Step 5 if needed.

### Step 5: Customize Visuals & Branding (2 minutes)

Gamma includes stock images, but you can improve them:

1. **For charts/data slides:**
   - Click the image placeholder
   - Search for a relevant chart type in Gamma's **image search**
   - Or, if you have specific data, describe what you need: "bar chart showing regional revenue breakdown"

2. **Brand colors (optional):**
   - If your company has specific colors, click **"Brand"** in Gamma
   - Upload a brand kit or manually set primary/secondary colors
   - Gamma will apply them across the deck

3. **Font adjustments (optional):**
   - Click **"Fonts"** in the top menu
   - Choose a professional font pair (Gamma has presets like "Modern," "Classic," "Bold")

4. **Export/download:**
   - Once satisfied, click **"Download"** or **"Export as PowerPoint"**
   - Save as `.pptx` (works with PowerPoint, Google Slides, Keynote)

## Step 6: Final Polish in PowerPoint or Keynote (optional, <1 minute)

If you need to make final tweaks:

1. Open the downloaded file in **PowerPoint**, **Google Slides**, or **Keynote**
2. Add speaker notes (click **Notes** section)
3. Insert any proprietary images or logos
4. Quick spell-check (Ctrl+F7 in PowerPoint)

**Optional:** If you want data-driven charts instead of stock images, insert your own Excel charts or copy live data from your dashboard.

## 3 Advanced Tips to Level Up Your AI Presentations

### Tip 1: Reuse Your Outline Template

Once you've created one presentation outline (like the Q3 Sales example), save it. For future presentations:
- Copy the outline structure
- Replace topic-specific data
- Feed it back to ChatGPT or Gamma
- You'll have a deck in 8 minutes next time

**Pro move:** Create a "template outline" document in Notion or Google Docs with your company's standard structure (intro → key metrics → findings → recommendations → next steps). Reuse it for every presentation.

### Tip 2: Use Gamma's "Regenerate Slide" Feature

If you don't like how a particular slide looks:
1. Select the slide
2. Click **"Regenerate"** or **"Redesign"**
3. Gamma will create 3 alternative layouts
4. Choose your favorite

This is faster than manually redesigning and perfect for slides that feel "off."

### Tip 3: Combine AI Content with Live Data

For data-heavy presentations (sales, analytics, performance reviews):

1. **Generate your narrative** with ChatGPT (talking points, conclusions)
2. **Use Gamma for structure** (layout, flow, design)
3. **Insert live charts** from Excel, Tableau, or Google Analytics
   - Download the chart as an image or link it directly
   - Paste into the relevant Gamma slide
   - Your deck now updates if the data source updates (especially useful in Google Slides)

This keeps your presentation both beautifully designed *and* data-accurate.
