The good news? Auto-generating meeting notes with AI is genuinely simple now. In this guide, I'll show you how to capture, transcribe, and organize meeting notes automatically — no coding, no complex setup, just three actual minutes of work.

## Step-by-Step: Auto-Generate Your Meeting Notes in 3 Minutes

### Step 1: Sign Up & Install (1 minute)

Go to **otter.ai** (or your chosen tool) and create a free account using your Google or Microsoft login. This takes 30 seconds.

Next, install the browser extension or mobile app:
- **Desktop users:** Add the Otter Chrome extension from the extension store
- **Mobile users:** Download the Otter app (iOS/Android)
- **Zoom/Teams users:** Authorize the integration in your meeting app settings

Done. No credit card, no complex onboarding.

### Step 2: Join Your Next Meeting with Recording Enabled (30 seconds)

Here's where the automation starts.

**If you're using Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams:**
1. Open your Otter app or browser extension
2. Click **"Start Recording"** before the meeting begins
3. Share the meeting link with attendees as normal

Otter will auto-detect that you're in a meeting and start capturing audio + transcription in real-time.

**If you're recording a phone call or external meeting:**
1. Click **"New Recording"** in the Otter app
2. Select **"Record a Meeting"** → the app will listen to your device audio
3. Start the call normally

The app runs in the background — you don't need to do anything during the meeting. Just talk.

### Step 3: Let AI Generate the Transcript (Automatic — < 1 minute)

Here's the magic: **Otter transcribes in real-time as the meeting happens.** You'll see the text appearing live in the app. By the time your 60-minute meeting ends, a full transcript is already there.

**Accuracy note:** Expect 85–95% accuracy on clear audio. If someone mumbles or has a thick accent, you might need to correct a few words, but that's still way faster than transcribing manually.

### Step 4: Let AI Extract Key Insights (1 minute)

Once the meeting ends, click **"Generate Summary"** or **"Extract Key Points."**

Otter (and Fireflies) use GPT-4 and Claude models to automatically:
- **Summarize** the meeting in 2–3 sentences
- **Extract action items** (tasks, deadlines, who's responsible)
- **Identify key decisions** made during the call
- **Flag important quotes** or moments

You'll see a formatted summary pop up in seconds. No manual work.

### Step 5: Customize & Share (1 minute)

Here's where you tailor it for your team:

1. **Add missing context:** If the AI missed something, click any line in the transcript to edit or add notes
2. **Tag teammates:** Use `@name` to assign action items to specific people
3. **Share the note:** Click **"Share"** and send the link, PDF, or paste it into Slack/Teams

Most tools let you set permissions (view-only vs. edit), so sensitive client calls stay private.

### Step 6: Optional — Auto-Send Summaries to Your Team

If you're using **Fireflies.ai Pro** ($10/month), enable **"Auto-send summaries"** in settings:
- Automatically post meeting summaries to Slack
- Include action items with assignees
- Send recap emails to attendees

This is the ultimate hands-off option. Your team gets notes without you lifting a finger.

## 3 Advanced Tips to Level Up

### Tip 1: Use AI-Generated Summaries as Meeting Minutes Templates

Instead of manually creating formal meeting notes, export your AI summary and paste it into a shared doc (Notion, Confluence, Google Docs). Use it as a starting point, then have one teammate review it for tone/accuracy. This cuts meeting documentation time by 80%.

### Tip 2: Create a Workflow with Zapier to Auto-Organize Notes

If you're managing 10+ meetings a week, connect Otter or Fireflies to Zapier:
- **Trigger:** Meeting ends
- **Action:** Auto-save transcript to Google Drive in a dated folder
- **Action:** Post summary to a Slack channel
- **Action:** Create a task in your project manager (Asana, Monday, etc.)

This is a one-time 5-minute setup that runs forever. [Automate Everything: Zapier + AI Workflows](/posts/2026-05-30-automate-everything-zapier-ai-workflows/) covers this in depth.

### Tip 3: Train Your AI Model on Your Team's Jargon

If your team uses industry-specific language, acronyms, or product names, both Otter and Fireflies let you add a **custom vocabulary list.** The AI will then recognize those terms more accurately.

Example: If you work in SaaS and say "ARR" (Annual Recurring Revenue) 50 times a meeting, upload a vocab file with your key terms. Accuracy jumps to 98%+.
